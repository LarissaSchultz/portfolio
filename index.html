about

Hey, I’m Larissa — a designer and illustrator.
My work moves between graphic design, art, and digital media, depending on what the idea needs. I’m interested in contrast, mood, and everything that doesn’t fit neatly into one category. Inspiration usually comes from everyday things — conversations, music, long walks, or just being online too late.
This site is a space for my work and ongoing ideas.
