interview

project description

This one-minute video features a compact interview with Angelina de Oliveira-Pump, a 65-year-old Catholic Brazilian woman who discusses her career and life journey. The project aimed to provide an authentic, respectful and careful insight into a personal story within a clear time frame.
The interview was created as part of a film-making course and focuses on clear questions and statements that portray the subject with dignity. I recorded, edited and post-produced it entirely myself. The content covers not only professional matters, but also identity, cultural background and personal perspectives.
